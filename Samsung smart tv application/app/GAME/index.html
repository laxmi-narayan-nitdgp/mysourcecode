rules

The game is played on a board consisting of several (initially empty) cells. Every player has a distinct color. Players take turns; each turn, a player must place an atom of his color to the game board. Atoms can be placed either to an empty cell, or to a cell which already contains your color. Every cell can contain a maximum of atoms equal to the number of its neighbors (4-side neighborhood). When the amount of atoms in a cell exceeds that cell's maximum, the cell explodes: one atom jumps to every neighbor. Jumping atoms instantly re-color all atoms in target cell to their own color. Player owning all cells on the board wins.
