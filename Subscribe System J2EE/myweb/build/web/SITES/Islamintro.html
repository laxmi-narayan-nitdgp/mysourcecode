A Brief Introduction to Islam

 

WHAT IS ISLAM ?

Islam comes from the Arabic word "Salam" which means peace. It is also derieved from the word Silm which means to submit your will to Almighty God. Therefore the word islam means peace acquired by submitting your will to Almighty God.

The word Muslim is somone who aquieres peace by submitting his or her will to Allah (The Almighty God).

 

There is a misconception that Islam came 1400 years ago and Prophet Muhammad(pbuh) was the founder of this religion. However Islam is since the day man put his foot on the earth and Prophet Muhammad (pbuh) was not the founder of Islam but the Last and Final Messenger of Allah (The Almighty God). Allah the Almighty says in his book "The Glorious Quran" as follows :

 

Muhammad is not the father of any of your men, but (he is) the Messenger of Allah, and the Seal of the Prophets: and Allah has full knowledge of all things. [Al Quran Chapter 33:40]

 

And We have sent you (O Muhammad pbuh): not but as a mercy for the 'Alamin (mankind, jinn and all that exists). [Al Quran Chapter 21:107]

 

And We have not sent you (O Muhammad pbuh) except as a giver of glad tidings and a warner to all mankind, but most of men know not. [Al Quran Chapter 34:28]

 

There were many prophets and messengers who came before the Last and Final Messenger as Allah (Almighty God) says in the glorious QUran. For each period was a book of guidance given to the Prophets and Messengers of that period just like the Torah to Moses (pbuh), Gospel to Jesus (pbuh) and Quran to Muhammad (pbuh). Allah the Almighty says in his book "The Glorious Quran" as follows :

 

We did send messengers before thee, and appointed for them wives and children: and it was never the part of a messenger to bring a sign except as Allah permitted (or commanded). For each period is a Book (revealed).
[Al Quran Chapter 13:38]

 

Verily We have sent you with the truth, a bearer of glad tidings and a warner. And there never was a nation but a warner had passed among them. [Al Quran Chapter 35:24]

 

You are only a warner, and to every people there is a guide. [Al Quran Chapter 13:7]

 

All the messengers and Prophets were given Books and revelation for people of their generation and for a particular time period but the Last and Final Messenger of Allah (The ALmighty God) was given Quran as a revelation and it was not for the Arabs or Muslims but for the whole mankind till the end of time. Allah the Almighty says in his book "The Glorious Quran" as follows :

 

Alif-Lam-Ra. [These letters are one of the miracles of the Qur'an, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings]. (This is) a Book which We have revealed unto you (O Muhammad pbuh) in order that you might lead mankind out of darkness (of disbelief and polytheism) into light (of belief in the Oneness of Allah and Islamic Monotheism) by their Lord's Leave to the Path of the All-Mighty, the Owner of all Praise.

[Al Quran Chapter 14 :1]

 

This (Qur'an) is a Message for mankind (and a clear proof against them), in order that they may be warned thereby, and that they may know that He is the only One Ilah (God - Allah) - (none has the right to be worshipped but Allah) - and that men of understanding may take heed. [Al Quran Chapter 14:52]

 

The month of Ramadan in which was revealed the Qur'an, a guidance for mankind and clear proofs for the guidance and the criterion (between right and wrong). [Al Quran Chapter 2:185]

 

Verily, We have sent down to you (O Muhammad pbuh) the Book (this Qur'an) for mankind in truth. So whosoever accepts the guidance, it is only for his ownself, and whosoever goes astray, he goes astray only for his (own) loss. And you (O Muhammad pbuh) are not a Wakil (trustee or disposer of affairs, or guardian) over them.
[Al Quran Chapter 39:41]

 

Islam does not believe in Anthropomorphism meaning that Almighty God takes human form so as to know the short comings of the humans and how humans feel when he or she is hurt or sad or in trouble etc and then The Almighty God can set the rules of DO's and Dont's for human beings after understanding them by taking human form.

 

Do they not relaise that the one who is the creater of everything know about all his creation and their needs in the heavens and the earth and all that exists ?

 

“….and it may be that you dislike a thing which is good for you and that you like a thing which is bad for you. Allah knows but you do not know.” [Al-Quran Al-1:216]

 

An examply to make things more clear, if a person makes or invents a device, he does not have to become that device to see how it works.Rather he would write an instruction manual just like Allah (The Amighty God) did in the form of Quran, an instrcution and guidance for mankind.

 

Furthermore if Amighty God came in human form to know the short comings of humans and how humans feel then does that mean he neglected the woman and female gender or he neglected the kids and old as most of the forms of Amighty God shown by people who follow Anthropomorphism is shown in a form of Strong Young Man. Therefore he would only know the short comings of a Man because as previously mentioned most of the people who believe in ANthropomorphism show Almighty as a man.

 

Muslims do not believe in Anthropomorphism. Allah is all seer and knower of everything hidden and apperent in theheavens and earth and has given us a book of guidance for mankind till the end of time.

 

"Whether you hide what is in your breasts or reveal it, Allah knows it, and He knows what is in the heavens and what is in the earth. And Allah is Able to do all things."

[Al-Quran, Chapter 3:29]
